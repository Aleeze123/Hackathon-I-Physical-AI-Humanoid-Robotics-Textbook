## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI, also known as Embodied AI, represents a paradigm shift from traditional AI, which primarily exists in software. It is the field of study dedicated to creating intelligent agents that have a physical body and can interact with their environment through sensors and actuators. These agents learn from and make decisions based on real-world experiences, bridging the gap between digital computation and physical reality.

### Key Concepts:

*   **Embodiment:** The idea that intelligence is not just about processing information but is deeply connected to having a physical body and experiencing the world through it.
*   **Perception:** How a robot or AI agent uses sensors (like cameras, LiDAR, and tactile sensors) to gather information about its surroundings.
*   **Action:** How an agent uses its actuators (motors, grippers, etc.) to effect change in its environment.
*   **Cognition:** The underlying computational processes that enable reasoning, planning, learning, and decision-making.
